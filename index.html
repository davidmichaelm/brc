In October 2018 Grace Lutheran Church in Milwaukee, WI challenged its members to read the Bible five days a week for a year. You're invited too!

This is a digital checklist designed to help you with that challenge. For best results, try setting this page as a bookmark on your mobile device, or maybe add it to your home screen. Reading the Bible isn't about checking things off a list, but a digital list like this one might help you keep track.

Visit the Grace Downtown website for more information on the challenge.
